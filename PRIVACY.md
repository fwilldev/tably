# Privacy Policy

**Tably** is a browser extension that replaces your new tab page. Your privacy is respected by design.

## Data Collection

Tably does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All settings, tab data, bookmarks, and history access remain entirely local in your browser using `chrome.storage`.

## External Connections

- **None on load.** Opening a new tab makes zero network requests.
- **AI Themes (optional):** If you choose to use the AI theme generator, you must provide your own OpenRouter API key. Requests are sent directly from your browser to OpenRouter's API. Tably does not proxy, store, or log these requests.
- **Ko-fi Widget (optional):** The settings panel loads a widget from `storage.ko-fi.com` to offer a support link. This only occurs when you open settings.

## Permissions

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `tabs` | Display and manage your open tabs |
| `storage` | Persist your settings locally |
| `history` | Search your browsing history from the new tab page |
| `bookmarks` | Search your bookmarks from the new tab page |

## Third Parties

Tably does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind. No data is shared with third parties.
